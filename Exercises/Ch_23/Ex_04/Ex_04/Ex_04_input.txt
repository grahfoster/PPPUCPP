This is easy and delicious. I made it with frozen haddock, which is
inexpensive. I used two pieces from a package that had about 6 or 8 pieces
and kept the rest in the freezer for another time. lmk if you want me to
send you some smoked paprika and bay leaves.

*Fish Chowder*

Dice and then saut=C3=A9 in Dutch oven in this order:

3 or 4 strips of bacon

1 small onion

1 carrot

2 large or 4 small potatoes

 Season with:

Salt and pepper

=C2=BD t smoked paprika

Deglaze pot with a little white wine or vermouth

Add:

1 can of corn, drained

2 bay leaves

Enough water to cover

Simmer until the liquid has reduced by a third and the potatoes are tender

Add and allow to thicken slightly:

About 1 c light cream (or milk or half and half)

Cut and stir into the soup:

=C2=BD lb. fish fillets

Simmer gently for five more minutes and serve
